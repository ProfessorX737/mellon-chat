Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Mellon Chat account, you have several options depending on how your account was set up.

Important: Account deletion is permanent. All your messages, room memberships, and account data will be removed from the homeserver. This action cannot be undone.

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open Mellon Chat on your device.
  2. Tap on Settings (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down to Account.
  4. Tap Security.
  5. Select Delete account.
  6. Enter your password to confirm.
  7. Tap Delete to permanently remove your account.

Method 2: Through Your Homeserver

If you registered your account on a public Matrix homeserver (such as matrix.org), you can also delete your account through the homeserver's own account management tools or admin interface.

Visit your homeserver's website and look for account settings or contact the homeserver administrator.

Method 3: Contact the Developer

If you are unable to delete your account through the methods above, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

xavierpoon737@gmail.com

Please include your Matrix user ID (e.g., @username:homeserver) in your request. We will process deletion requests within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (display name, avatar).
  • Your room memberships.
  • Your device and encryption keys.
  • Any locally cached data on your device (after uninstalling the app).

Note: Messages you have already sent in rooms with other participants may remain visible to those participants, as they are stored on their homeservers according to the Matrix federation protocol.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at xavierpoon737@gmail.com.
