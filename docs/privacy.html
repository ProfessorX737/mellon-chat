Mellon Chat

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Mellon Chat is an open-source Matrix chat client. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Mellon Chat, the following data may be processed:

  • Account information: Your Matrix user ID, display name, and avatar (if set).
  • Messages: Text messages, media, and files you send through the app.
  • Device information: Device keys used for end-to-end encryption verification.
  • Usage metadata: Timestamps and room membership as required by the Matrix protocol.

3. End-to-End Encryption

Mellon Chat supports end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for private conversations. When encryption is enabled, message content is encrypted on your device before being sent. Neither the homeserver operator nor the Mellon Chat developers can read encrypted messages.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on the Matrix homeserver you choose to connect to. Mellon Chat itself does not operate any servers or store your data externally. You are responsible for reviewing the privacy practices of your chosen homeserver provider.

Some data (encryption keys, session tokens) is cached locally on your device to enable the app to function. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

Mellon Chat does not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. We do not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in the app.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data associated with your Matrix account.
  • Delete your account and associated data (see Account Deletion).
  • Export your data through standard Matrix protocol tools.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. For detailed instructions, please visit our Account Deletion page.

8. Children's Privacy

Mellon Chat is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
